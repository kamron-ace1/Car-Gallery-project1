Heritage

A Century of Innovation

"Born from aviation, driven by passion."

Bayerische Motoren Werke (BMW) was founded in 1916 in Munich, Germany. From aircraft engines to the world's most desirable automobiles, our journey has been defined by relentless innovation and the pursuit of perfection.

The iconic kidney grille, the Hofmeister kink, and the legendary inline-six engine – these are not just design elements, but symbols of a brand that has shaped automotive history for over a century.
